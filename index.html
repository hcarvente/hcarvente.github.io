About Me

Leadership, accountability, and a commitment to keep growing.

My journey has not been linear. I am directly impacted by the justice system, and the experiences that shaped my early life forced me to reflect deeply on my choices, my environment, and the kind of person I wanted to become. Over time, I made a conscious decision to build a different path, not only for myself, but for the communities I come from.

Over the last several years, I have worked across juvenile justice reform, youth leadership development, nonprofit strategy, healing-centered work, and community partnership building. Today, I serve as Executive Director of Alianza for Opportunity, where I help develop emerging leaders from Latine, Afro-Latine, and Indigenous communities.

I am now expanding my skills into software engineering and AI because I want to create practical tools that help mission-driven organizations, improve access to information, and better serve impacted communities.

My Journey

Turning lived experience into leadership and purpose.

2008–2014

Accountability and transformation

I was incarcerated from June 2008 to June 2012 and came off parole in June 2014. During that time, I met a mentor who helped me develop critical thinking, discipline, and the confidence to face fears around writing, speaking, and stepping into new spaces.

2013–2022

Justice reform and youth leadership

I worked with organizations and public agencies focused on improving conditions of confinement, closing youth prisons, and ensuring young people and families had a meaningful role in system transformation.

2020–Present

Entrepreneurship and healing work

Through Healing Ninjas, I explored ways to destigmatize mental health and imagine technology-supported wellness access rooted in culture and community.

2023–Present

Leading nationally and pivoting into tech

As Executive Director of Alianza for Opportunity, I have strengthened my communication, collaboration, and problem-solving skills. I am now building technical skills so I can pair leadership with software and AI tools.
